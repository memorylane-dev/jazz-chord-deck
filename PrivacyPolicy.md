# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-07

ChordDeck is a jazz chord flashcard app for iOS. This Privacy Policy explains how ChordDeck handles information.

## Data Collection

ChordDeck does not collect personal data.

The app does not require an account, does not use advertising, does not use analytics, and does not include third-party tracking SDKs.

## Local Data

ChordDeck stores practice-related settings locally on your device, including selected roots, selected chord qualities, practice order, session length, autoplay preference, and marked cards.

This local data is used only to make the app remember your practice setup and difficult cards. It is not sent to the developer or to a third party by ChordDeck.

## Audio

ChordDeck uses on-device audio playback to play chord tones. Audio playback does not record microphone input and does not send audio data off your device.

## Children

ChordDeck is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if ChordDeck's data practices change. The updated policy will be posted at the same location with a new "Last updated" date.
