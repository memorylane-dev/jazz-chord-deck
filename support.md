# ChordDeck Support

Thank you for using ChordDeck.

ChordDeck is a compact iOS flashcard app for practicing jazz chord recall. It helps you drill selected roots and chord qualities, reveal chord tones and formulas, hear chord playback, and review marked cards.

## Common Questions

### Does ChordDeck require an account?

No. ChordDeck does not require an account.

### Does ChordDeck collect personal data?

No. The current version does not collect personal data, use analytics, show ads, or include third-party tracking SDKs.

### Where are practice settings stored?

Practice settings and marked cards are stored locally on your device.

### Can I review difficult cards?

Yes. Mark difficult cards during practice, then start a marked-card review deck from the first screen.

### Why does chord spelling use flat note names?

The current version uses a compact flat-name spelling model for fast flashcard practice. Future versions may add more advanced enharmonic spelling options.

### What should I do if audio does not play?

Check that your device volume is up and Silent Mode is off. If the issue continues, include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue when contacting support.

## Troubleshooting

If ChordDeck is not behaving as expected:

1. Force close and reopen the app.
2. Check that your device is running a recent iOS version.
3. Contact support with the steps that caused the issue.
